# South Korea · Semiconductors and internet platforms

Back to **[Tech giants overview](/en/companies/overview)**.

## Companies

- **[Samsung Electronics](/en/companies/korea/samsung)** — Global semiconductor and consumer electronics leader; world's #1 smartphone vendor since 2012; DRAM/NAND flash dominant; Galaxy S/Z series, TVs, home appliances.
- **[SK hynix](/en/companies/korea/sk-hynix)** — World's #2 memory semiconductor (DRAM + NAND); HBM supplier to Nvidia for AI; key nodes: Hyundai Electronics origin (1983), 2012 SK Group acquisition, 2021 Intel NAND purchase.
- **[Naver](/en/companies/korea/naver)** — Korea's dominant search engine (~75% market share); internet conglomerate with Webtoon, Wattpad, Poshmark; Knowledge iN Q&A pioneer.
- **[Kakao](/en/companies/korea/kakao)** — Internet platform centered on KakaoTalk (~93% Korea messaging penetration); Kakao Bank, Kakao Pay, Kakao T mobility, Kakao Entertainment.
- **LG Electronics** — Consumer electronics and home appliances; mobile phones, TVs, refrigerators, washing machines; part of LG Group.
