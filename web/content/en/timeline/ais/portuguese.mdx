# Portuguese — Brazilian and Iberian Markets

Portuguese (Português) connects two major markets: Brazil, the largest country in South America with a thriving tech scene, and Portugal, gateway to European Union. With approximately 85-100% of English token cost, Portuguese offers Latin-script efficiency with access to over 260 million native speakers.

## Portuguese Token Characteristics

**Romance Language Structure**

Portuguese shares Romance language features with Spanish and French:

- Verb conjugation: eu falo, tu falas, ele fala, nós falamos, vocês falam
- Gender agreement: o programador, a programadora
- Rich verbal system with multiple tenses

**Token/Character Ratio**

```
Portuguese: "concatenação" → 3-4 tokens (12 characters)
English: "concatenation" → 1 token (13 characters)

Portuguese: "inteligência artificial" → 4-5 tokens
English: "artificial intelligence" → 3 tokens
```

Portuguese and Spanish have similar token efficiency profiles, with minor differences in specific constructions.

## Best Practices for Portuguese Prompts

### Do

- **Use Anglicismos técnicos** — English tech terms work well in both European and Brazilian contexts
- **Keep constructions direct** — "escreve" (write), "corrige" (fix) are concise
- **Prefer European Portuguese neutrality** — less slang, more universally understood
- **Use "você" over "tu"** — Brazilian dominance in training data

### Avoid

- **Excessive informal register** — "cara" (buddy) adds no value
- **Regional slang mixing** — Portuguese-Brazilian slang differs significantly from European
- **Overly formal constructions** — "venha por favor" wastes tokens

## Brazilian vs European Portuguese

| Aspect | Brazilian (BR) | European (PT) |
|--------|---------------|---------------|
| Token Efficiency | ~90-100% | ~85-95% |
| AI Comprehension | Very High | Medium-High |
| Tech Community Size | Large | Medium |
| Verb Form (you) | você | você (formal) or tu |

<Callout type="warning">
  Brazilian Portuguese dominates AI training data due to population size. European Portuguese constructions may be less reliably understood.
</Callout>

## Example Prompts

**Efficient (Recommended):**
```
Escreve um quicksort em Python
```
Tokens: ~7 | Meaning: Write quicksort in Python

**Verbose (Less Efficient):**
```
Será que você poderia, por obséquio, escrever-me um algoritmo de ordenação rápida na linguagem Python? Ficaria muito grato!
```
Tokens: ~30 | Meaning: Same result, 4x tokens

## Conclusion

Portuguese offers 85-100% of English token cost with access to Brazilian and European markets. For Portuguese speakers:

- Brazilian Portuguese is more reliably understood by AI
- English technical terms integrate well
- Keep prompts concise and direct
- Regional variations exist but don't significantly impact efficiency
