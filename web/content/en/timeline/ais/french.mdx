# French — European and African Reach

French (Français) occupies a significant position in global AI-assisted programming as both a major European language and the most widely spoken language in Africa. With approximately 80-95% of English token cost, French offers a middle ground between Latin-script efficiency and Romance language expressiveness.

## French Token Characteristics

**Romance Language Structure**

French is a Romance language with rich morphological system:

- Verb conjugation: je parle, tu parles, il parle, nous parlons, vous parlez, ils parlent
- Gender agreement: le programmeur, la programmeuse
- Complex negation: ne...pas, ne...jamais

**Token/Character Ratio**

```
French: "concaténation" → 3-4 tokens (12 characters)
English: "concatenation" → 1 token (13 characters)

French: "intelligence artificielle" → 3-4 tokens
English: "artificial intelligence" → 3 tokens
```

French typically requires 15-20% more tokens than English for equivalent content due to longer word forms and more complex morphology.

## Best Practices for French Prompts

### Do

- **Use Anglicismes techniques** — English tech terms (API, SQL, JavaScript) work well in French contexts
- **Employ formal register** — "veuillez" (please), "merci de" (thank you for) add politeness efficiently
- **Keep imperatives direct** — "corrige" (fix), "écris" (write) are concise
- **Use French technical vocabulary** — "tableau" (array), "chaîne" (string) are well understood

### Avoid

- **Excessive politesse** — overly formal French constructions waste tokens
- **Anglo-French hybrid** — mixing English and French inconsistently reduces efficiency
- **Subjonctif overuse** — "que je puisse" adds tokens; use indicatif when possible

## French in Africa

French is the official language of 29 African countries and the most widely spoken language on the continent. This gives French AI prompts access to:

- Growing tech markets in West and Central Africa
- French-language documentation for open source projects
- International organizations (UN, EU, AU)

<Callout type="warning">
  African French has distinctive vocabulary and expressions. AI models trained primarily on European French may not fully understand African French variations.
</Callout>

## Conclusion

French offers 80-95% of English token cost with significant global reach across Europe and Africa. For French speakers, the recommendation is to:

- Embrace technical Anglicismes where they improve efficiency
- Keep prompts concise and direct
- Use formal register for professional contexts
- Consider French African variations for broader audiences
