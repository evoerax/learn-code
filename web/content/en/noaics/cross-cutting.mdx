# Cross-cutting & applied computing

Using computational methods in **other sciences or industry systems**: highly applied, but **algorithms, data, systems, and domain knowledge** all matter.

---

## Example domains

| Domain | Focus |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Comp bio / bioinformatics** | Sequences, structures, evolution, high-throughput data |
| **Digital health & medical informatics** | Clinical data, imaging, privacy & regulation |
| **Computational finance** | HFT, risk, market microstructure (systems/math crossover) |
| **Smart cities & mobility** | Simulation, optimization, multi-source fusion |
| **Industrial software & digital twins** | CAD/CAE pipelines, PLM, IoT/CPS hooks |

---

## Boundary with “AI-only”

Many applications use **ML**, but **problem definition, data, evaluation, and deployment** remain **domain + systems** work. This section treats **cross-cutting tracks** as independent research and hiring paths, not “everything is AI.”
