# Computer graphics & visualization

Seeing and **synthesizing / processing** geometric and image content—games, film, CAD, digital twins, robotics simulation.

---

## Sub-areas

| Topic | Typical questions |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Modeling & rendering** | Rasterization, path tracing, materials, real-time pipelines |
| **Geometry** | Meshes, volumes, surfaces & topology |
| **Animation & simulation** | Physics, characters, fluids |
| **Scientific / info visualization** | Scalar fields, graphs & networks, immersive analytics |
| **XR & spatial computing** | Borders [HCI](/en/noaics/hci) and robotics perception |

---

## Overlap with AI

**Neural rendering** and **generative 3D** overlap models; **classical graphics** (sampling, reconstruction, geometry algorithms) remains its own conference and curriculum track. Industrial visualization and digital twins keep demand high even when public hype focuses on generation.
