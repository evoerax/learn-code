# Robotics, embedded systems, & cyber-physical systems (CPS)

A **major corridor** overlapping AI (perception, planning, learning control) but with **real-time, safety-critical, and mechatronic integration** as first-class concerns.

---

## Sub-areas

| Topic | Typical questions |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Embedded & real-time** | Predictable scheduling, WCET, RTOS, hardware co-design |
| **Sensing & estimation** | Filtering, fusion, state estimation (ties to [control](/en/hardware/basics/control)) |
| **Robotic systems** | Integration, motion planning, multi-robot coordination |
| **Unmanned systems** | Air/ground/space, regulation & mission constraints |
| **CPS** | Coupling computation and physical dynamics, safety & reliability |

---
