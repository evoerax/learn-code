# Human–computer interaction (HCI)

How people cooperate with computational systems **naturally, efficiently, and trustworthily**; overlaps design, cognitive science, and social science.

---

## Sub-areas

| Topic | Typical questions |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Interaction & usability** | Task analysis, prototyping, evaluation methods |
| **Multimodal & XR** | Voice, gestures, AR/VR, spatial computing |
| **Collaboration & groups** | CSCW, co-editing, awareness & permissions |
| **Visualization** | Information visualization (border with [graphics](/en/noaics/graphics)) |
| **Trust & accessibility** | Explainable experience, a11y, fairness & ethics in interaction |

---

## Overlap with AI

Shipping LLM products surfaces problems in **intent, control, feedback, workflows, and trust**—often mislabeled as “model issues” but fundamentally **HCI**. [PACM HCI](https://dl.acm.org/journal/pacmhci) remains a flagship venue.
