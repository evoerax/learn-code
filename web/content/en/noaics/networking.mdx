# Computer networking

How data moves and systems interconnect—cloud, IoT, and mobile stacks all depend on this layer.

---

## Sub-areas

| Topic | Typical questions |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Protocols & performance** | Reliable transport, congestion control, QUIC/TCP, application semantics |
| **Data-center networks** | Topologies, load balancing, RDMA, low-latency RPC |
| **Cloud & edge** | CDNs, edge nodes, 5G/6G access and backhaul |
| **Wireless & mobile** | Wi‑Fi, cellular, ad hoc, massive connectivity |
| **Measurement & optimization** | Traffic engineering, active/passive measurement, programmable networks |

---

## Overlap with AI

Large-model training and inference are extremely sensitive to **bandwidth and latency**; **interconnects for distributed training** span systems and networking. The **networking discipline** (protocols, routing, topology) remains distinct from “ML infra” as a job title, even when they meet.
