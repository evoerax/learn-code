# AI software stack

This section focuses on **software above the hardware**: libraries and runtimes that turn models into trainable artifacts, **deployable services**, **agentic workflows**, and **measurable systems**.

It complements **[AI models](/en/ais/overview)** (what shipped), **[AI datasets](/en/datasets/overview)** (benchmarks and corpora), and **[AGI stack](/en/agi/overview)** (architectural themes). For **language-level history**, see **[Programming languages](/en/timeline)**; for **electronics-to-robots hardware path**, see **[Hardware](/en/hardware/overview)**; for **product and cloud operators**, see **[Tech giants](/en/companies/overview)**.

| Topic | What belongs here |

## Boundary (intentional blur)

- **Models vs software:** a *weight release* lives under **AI models**; the *default way people fine-tune or serve it* lives here.
- **Software vs companies:** vendor roadmaps and business context stay under **Tech giants**; portable open-source layers and patterns stay here unless the page is explicitly company-scoped.
