# Whole Systems

The **Systems** section (整机脉络) has three **second-level entry points** in the sidebar, each with **third-level topic pages** for drilling into subsystems.

---

## Three Whole-System Tracks

| Track | Entry | Third-Level Focus |
|-------|-------|-------------------|
| Aerospace dynamics + high-performance electric propulsion | [Drones](/en/hardware/systems/drones) | Airframe & propulsion, flight control loops, link & safety |
| Automotive-grade electrical + drive-by-wire + ADAS | [Automotive](/en/hardware/systems/automotive) | Onboard networks, drive-by-wire, ADAS sensors |
| Mechanism + servo + collaborative/logistics | [Robotics](/en/hardware/systems/robotics) | Manipulators & joints, mobile bases, system integration & safety |
