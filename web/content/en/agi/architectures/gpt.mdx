# GPT Series

OpenAI's Generative Pre-trained Transformer series.

---

## Evolution

| Model | Year | Params | Key Innovation |
|-------|------|--------|----------------|
| **GPT-1** | 2018 | 117M | Decoder-only, pretrain + fine-tune |
| **GPT-2** | 2019 | 1.5B | Zero-shot transfer |
| **GPT-3** | 2020 | 175B | In-context learning |
| **GPT-3.5** | 2022 | ? | RLHF, ChatGPT |
| **GPT-4** | 2023 | ? | Multimodal, instruction following |
| **GPT-4o** | 2024 | ? | Native multimodality |
| **o1** | 2024 | ? | Reasoning, chain of thought |

## In-Context Learning

GPT-3's key insight: large models learn tasks from prompts without gradient updates.

```
Task: Translate English to French

Prompt:
English: hello
French: bonjour
English: goodbye
French: 

Model: au revoir
```

No fine-tuning needed - just show examples in context!
