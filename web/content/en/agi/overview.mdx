This section is a **2026-oriented** frame for tracing ideas from **Transformer and MoE** toward long-context operators, System-2 reasoning, multimodal fusion, and embodied AI— alongside the site’s **[AI Models](/en/ais/overview)** timeline.

## Evolution snapshot

| Phase | Representative ideas | Main problem addressed |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Perception** | Transformer, BERT, ViT | Cross-modal features and alignment |
| **Scale-up** | MoE (Mixture of Experts) | Large effective parameters within a power envelope |
| **Long sequence** | SSM (Mamba family), FlashAttention, linear/hybrid attention | Cost of long docs/video and KV footprint |
| **Logic** | RLHF / DPO, CoT, MCTS, test-time compute | Planning, verification, “slow thinking” |
| **Embodiment** | World models, JEPA, VLA | Physics and acting in the real world |

## Do not skip in 2026: world models

**World models** aim to internalize **causal and physical structure** (what happens if you let go of a cup), not only token statistics. They tie together predictive video/interaction objectives (JEPA-style ideas) and **vision–language–action (VLA)** stacks for robotics.
