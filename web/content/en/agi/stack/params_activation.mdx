# Parameters vs activated parameters

- **Total params**: Full weight count—“capacity.”  
- **Activated params** (or active FLOPs per token): the **subset actually used in one forward**; dense models: near-total; **MoE**: huge total, small activated subset.  
- **Use**: For comparable **compute budgets**, activated metrics usually track latency and $/token better than headline param count.
