# FlashAttention & fast attention kernels

- **FlashAttention**: Tiled computation and reduced HBM↔SRAM traffic—**numerically standard attention** with big speedups / VRAM savings.  
- **Lineage**: FlashAttention-2/3 iterations target occupancy and hardware efficiency.  
- **In practice**: Often combined with **GQA / MQA / MLA** and packed variable-length batches; ties directly to [KV cache](/en/agi/stack/kv_cache) pressure.
