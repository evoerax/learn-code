## How L1s compare

| Feature | Ethereum | Solana | BNB |
|---------|----------|--------|-----|
| **Smart contracts** | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| **EVM compatible** | Native | No | Yes |
| **Rollup centric** | Yes | No | No |
| **Decentralization** | High | Growing | Moderate |
| **Throughput** | Low (L1) | High | Medium |
| **Finality** | ~12 min | ~0.4 sec | ~1 sec |
