# China Flagship Models (2025–2026)

## Overview

Chinese AI labs take a different approach: **most models are open-weight or open-source with fully disclosed parameters**. This transparency has made them critical for AI research, especially for studying MoE scaling laws.

<Callout type="info" emoji="🇨🇳">
**Key Pattern:** Chinese labs (Alibaba, Baidu, DeepSeek, Tencent, 01.AI) → parameters typically disclosed. ByteDance, Zhipu, iFlytek → often undisclosed.
</Callout>

## Key Observations

### MoE Dominates Chinese Large Models

<Callout type="default" emoji="🏗️">
**MoE Architecture Pattern:** Chinese labs aggressively adopt Mixture-of-Experts:
- DeepSeek-V3: 671B total / 37B active (12x efficiency)
- Qwen3.5: 397B total / 17B active (~23x efficiency)
- ERNIE 4.5: 424B total / 47B active (~9x efficiency)
</Callout>

### Why Chinese Labs Disclose Parameters

1. **Research community**: Open-source culture from academia (Tsinghua, Zhipu)
2. **Competitive differentiation**:透明度 as a selling point for enterprise adoption
3. **MoE research**: Clear parameter counts help the research community study scaling laws
4. **Regulatory environment**: Different posture compared to US labs
