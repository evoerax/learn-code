# 2020: The Era of Large Models Begins

## Overview

**2020** marked a quantum leap with GPT-3's 175 billion parameters. The paper "Language Models are Few-Shot Learners" showed that massive scale alone could enable few-shot learning —完成任务 with just a few examples in context.

## Key Milestone

### GPT-3 (OpenAI)
- **Parameters**: 175 billion
- **Architecture**: 96-layer Transformer, 96 attention heads, 12288 embedding dimension
- **Training Data**: CommonCrawl, WebText, Wikipedia, Books
- **Context Window**: 2048 tokens
- **Key Innovation**: Few-shot learning via in-context examples

<Callout type="success" emoji="🚀">
GPT-3 was ~10x larger than any previous non-sparse language model. It demonstrated that scale + prompting could replace task-specific training.
</Callout>
