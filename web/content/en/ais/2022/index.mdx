# 2022: The Year AI Went Mainstream

## Overview

**2022** was a pivotal year. Google's PaLM demonstrated that dense transformers could scale to 540 billion parameters. China's Tsinghua University open-sourced GLM-130B. But the real story was November 30 — when OpenAI released ChatGPT and changed the world.

<Callout type="success" emoji="🚀">
ChatGPT reached 1 million users in 5 days — faster than any technology in history.
</Callout>

## Key Milestones

### ChatGPT (OpenAI) — November 30, 2022
- **Release Date**: November 30, 2022
- **Based on**: GPT-3.5 (fine-tuned for dialogue)
- **Significance**: Sparked global AI race, fastest user adoption in history

### PaLM (Google)
- **Parameters**: 540 billion
- **Architecture**: Dense Transformer
- **Significance**: Set new benchmarks on reasoning, joke explanation, code generation

### GLM-130B (Tsinghua / Zhipu AI)
- **Parameters**: 130 billion
- **Architecture**: General Language Model (GLM)
- **Significance**: Early high-quality open-source Chinese LLM
