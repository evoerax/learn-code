# 🗓️ Large model timeline (2017–2026)

> Highlights by year; figures and capabilities follow public papers and vendor posts. Cross-year programs are filed by first broad availability.

For **architecture families and capability pillars** (Transformer, MoE, SSM/hybrid blocks, test-time compute, world models, embodiment), see **[AGI stack](/en/agi/overview)**. For **frameworks, serving, agents, and eval tooling**, see **[AI software](/en/software/overview)**.

Body is **2026 → 2017** (newest first). Each year lists **model name** then **one-sentence takeaway**.

---

## 2026 (outlook)

> Expectations only—verify on vendor channels.

**GPT-6 / GPT-5.5 (OpenAI, expected)**

Native multimodal planning plus System‑2-style reasoning fused with tool chains toward general agents.

**Gemini 3.0 (Google, expected)**

Very long context, multitask scheduling, and on-device/cloud stack; world-model and embodied threads.

**Claude 5.0 (Anthropic, expected)**

Stronger alignment and auditable reasoning; constitutional AI and dynamic human-preference modeling.

**DeepSeek-R3 / V4 (DeepSeek, expected)**

MoE + reasoning chains + dynamic sparsity; token economics and open-weight strategy stay in focus.

**Qwen4 / Qwen-Omni (Alibaba, expected)**

Balances parameter efficiency, lightweight deployment, licenses, and unified text/image/audio/video modeling.

**Llama 5 (Meta, expected)**

Open multimodal line expanding toward 3D, embodied interaction, and multi-device workflows.

**Mistral 5 (Mistral AI, expected)**

Multilingual flagship plus SMB tooling and edge/privacy-oriented packaging.

**Open Source AGI Initiative (community, expected)**

Multi-org open mega-model narrative stressing transparent training, reproducible evals, and governance.

## 🔑 Evolution summary

| Phase | Years | Focus | Examples |
|-------|-------|-------|----------|
| Architecture | 2017 | Attention | Transformer |
| Pre-training | 2018–19 | MLM / text-to-text | BERT, GPT-2, T5 |
| Scale | 2020–21 | scaling laws + ICL | GPT-3, PaLM, GLM-130B |
| Alignment | 2022 | RLHF + constitutional AI | ChatGPT, InstructGPT, Claude |
| Multimodal | 2023–24 | native multimodal + long ctx | GPT-4V, Gemini, Qwen-VL |
| Efficiency / reasoning | 2024–25 | MoE + test-time compute | DeepSeek-V3, o1, Mixtral |
| Open ecosystem | 2023–26 | weights + efficient deploy | Llama, Qwen, Mistral |
| Outlook | 2026+ | agents / world models | TBD |

> **Trend**: parameter races → reasoning & efficiency → native multimodal → open ecosystems → agents & general tooling.
