# 2025: The Most Capable AI Yet

## Overview

**2025** delivered GPT-5 — OpenAI's "most intelligent, fastest, most useful model yet" — along with o3-pro, GPT-4.5 "Orion", and Agent capabilities. Chinese labs continued MoE innovation with Qwen3 and DeepSeek-V3.

<Callout type="success" emoji="🚀">
2025 was the year AI became genuinely useful for real-world work, with Agent frameworks and advanced reasoning becoming mainstream.
</Callout>
