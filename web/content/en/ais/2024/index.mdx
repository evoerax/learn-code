# 2024: Breakthrough Year

## Overview

**2024** was the most consequential year in AI history. OpenAI released reasoning models (o1), multimodal omni-models (GPT-4o), and video generation (Sora). Chinese labs demonstrated MoE efficiency with DeepSeek-V3 and Qwen 2.5.

<Callout type="success" emoji="🚀">
2024 saw the first "thinking" models (o1), text-to-video (Sora), and the Chinese MoE breakthrough.
</Callout>

## OpenAI 2024 Timeline

| Date | Model | Key Feature |
|------|-------|-------------|
| Feb 16 | **Sora** | Text-to-video, 20-second clips |
| May 13 | **GPT-4o** | Multimodal, audio output |
| Jul 18 | **GPT-4o mini** | Faster, cheaper |
| Jul 25 | **SearchGPT** | Real-time web search |
| Sep 12 | **o1-preview / o1-mini** | Reasoning models |
| Oct 3 | **GPT-4o with Canvas** | Interactive coding/writing |
| Nov 7 | **GPT-4 Turbo** | 128K context, lower cost |
| Dec 5 | **o1** | Full reasoning model |
