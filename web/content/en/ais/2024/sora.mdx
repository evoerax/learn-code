# Sora

> _Text-to-video, 20 seconds at a time_

## At a Glance

| | |
|---|---|
| **Release Date** | February 16, 2024 |
| **Organization** | OpenAI |
| **Key Innovation** | Text-to-video generation, 20-second clips |
| **Status** | Released in "12 Days of OpenAI" (Dec 2024) |

## Overview

Sora was OpenAI's text-to-video model capable of generating realistic, imaginative video scenes from text instructions. Initially announced in February 2024, it wasn't publicly released until December.

<Callout type="info" emoji="🎬">
Sora could generate videos up to 20 seconds long, with complex scenes, multiple characters, and dynamic camera movements.
</Callout>

## Key Capabilities

- **Text-to-video**: Generate videos from text descriptions
- **Duration**: Up to 20 seconds
- **Style options**: Realistic, animated, artistic
- **Extensions**: Extend existing videos, animate images

## Impact

Sora represented a major step toward AI-generated content, joining DALL-E (images) and GPT (text). It raised both excitement and concerns about synthetic media.
