## Origin Story

Gemini 1.5 Pro introduced a breakthrough: **1 million token context window**, allowing the model to process entire books, codebases, or video transcripts in a single prompt.

<Callout type="info" emoji="📜">
The million-token context was a 32x improvement over GPT-4's ~128K context, enabling entirely new use cases like analyzing entire codebases.
</Callout>

## Key Capabilities

- **1 million token context**: Can process entire books, codebases, hours of video
- **Multimodal**: Text, images, audio, video understanding
- **Long context reasoning**: Maintains coherence across massive contexts
