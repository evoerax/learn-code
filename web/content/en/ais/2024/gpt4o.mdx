## Overview

GPT-4o ("o" for omni) was a breakthrough in multimodal AI. Unlike previous models that used separate components for different modalities, GPT-4o processed audio, vision, and text in a unified model.

<Callout type="success" emoji="🎯">
GPT-4o could respond to audio inputs in as little as 232ms — similar to human conversation response time.
</Callout>

## Key Capabilities

- **Real-time audio**: Natural conversation with voice, music, sound effects
- **Vision**: Analyze images, documents, charts in real-time
- **Multimodal**: Single model handles text, audio, and images
- **Speed**: 2x faster than GPT-4 Turbo, 50% cheaper
