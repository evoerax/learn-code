## Why Parameters Are Disclosed

Unlike closed-source models, Meta openly publishes parameter counts as a competitive advantage:
- Helps enterprise planning (hardware requirements)
- Enables research community to study scaling laws
- Differentiates from OpenAI/Google secrecy
