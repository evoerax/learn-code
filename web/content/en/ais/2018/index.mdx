# 2018: The Beginning of the GPT Era

## Overview

**2018** marked the birth of the GPT (Generative Pre-Trained) paradigm. OpenAI demonstrated that large-scale unsupervised pre-training followed by task-specific fine-tuning could revolutionize natural language understanding.

## Key Milestone

### GPT-1 (OpenAI)
- **Parameters**: ~117 million
- **Architecture**: 12-layer Transformer decoder
- **Training Data**: BooksCorpus (4.5 GB, ~7,000 unique books)
- **Significance**: First to prove "pre-training + fine-tuning" works

<Callout type="info" emoji="💡">
This was the first demonstration that scaling transformers on large text corpora could learn general language representations transferable to downstream tasks.
</Callout>
