# GPT-1

> _"Improving Language Understanding by Generative Pre-Training"_

## Origin Story

In 2018, OpenAI published a paper demonstrating that **pre-training a large language model on diverse text, then fine-tuning on specific tasks** could achieve remarkable results. This two-stage approach became the foundation of modern NLP.

<Callout type="info" emoji="💡">
GPT-1 proved that scale + pre-training was the path forward for language understanding — a paradigm that still dominates today.
</Callout>

## Architecture

- **12-layer Transformer decoder**
- **117 million parameters**
- **Context length**: 512 tokens
- **Training data**: BooksCorpus — 4.5 GB of text from ~7,000 unique books

## Key Innovation

**Pre-training + Fine-tuning**: Unlike previous approaches that trained on each task from scratch, GPT-1:

1. Pre-trained on large unlabeled corpus (unsupervised)
2. Fine-tuned on specific tasks with small labeled datasets
3. Transferred learned representations to downstream tasks
