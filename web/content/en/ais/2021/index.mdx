# 2021: Chinese LLMs Enter the Race

## Overview

**2021** saw Chinese AI labs emerge with large-scale models. Baidu's ERNIE 3.0 Titan demonstrated knowledge-enhanced pre-training, setting records as one of the largest Chinese dense models at 260 billion parameters.

## Key Milestone

### ERNIE 3.0 Titan (Baidu)
- **Parameters**: 260 billion
- **Architecture**: Knowledge-enhanced Dense Transformer
- **Training**: Large-scale Chinese + multilingual corpus with knowledge integration
- **Significance**: Largest Chinese dense model at the time, strong Chinese language understanding

<Callout type="info" emoji="🇨🇳">
ERNIE 3.0 Titan pioneered knowledge-enhanced pre-training in China, integrating structured knowledge into the pre-training process.
</Callout>
