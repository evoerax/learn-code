## Origin Story

In December 2021, Baidu released ERNIE 3.0 Titan — at 260 billion parameters, one of the largest Chinese dense models. The key innovation was integrating **structured knowledge** directly into the pre-training process.

<Callout type="info" emoji="🇨🇳">
ERNIE 3.0 Titan was a major milestone for Chinese AI, demonstrating that China could train at GPT-3 scale with additional knowledge integration.
</Callout>

## Architecture

- **260 billion parameters** (dense Transformer)
- Knowledge-enhanced pre-training with structured knowledge graphs
- Multi-task learning for unified understanding and generation
