## Origin Story

Claude 2 emerged as Anthropic's response to GPT-4, emphasizing safety, helpfulness, and the Constitutional AI approach — training models to align with human values through a set of guiding principles.

<Callout type="info" emoji="🛡️">
Anthropic's focus on safety didn't come at the cost of capability — Claude 2 was competitive with GPT-4 on many benchmarks.
</Callout>

## Constitutional AI

Claude 2 was trained using Constitutional AI, which:

1. Uses a set of principles to evaluate outputs
2. Self-critiques and revises responses
3. Reduces harmful outputs while maintaining helpfulness
