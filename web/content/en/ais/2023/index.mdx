# 2023: The Foundation Year

## Overview

**2023** was the year AI truly began. Every major lab launched flagship models. OpenAI released GPT-4. Anthropic launched Claude. Meta open-sourced Llama. Mistral surprised everyone with efficient architectures. Chinese labs (Qwen, ERNIE, DeepSeek) entered the race.

<Callout type="success" emoji="🏆">
2023 proved that the AI race was truly global — with major players in the US, China, and Europe.
</Callout>
