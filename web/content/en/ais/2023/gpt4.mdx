## Origin Story

GPT-4 represented a dramatic leap in capabilities, approaching human-level performance on professional exams and complex reasoning tasks. OpenAI kept parameters secret, focusing on capabilities over architecture.

<Callout type="success" emoji="🎯">
GPT-4 was widely considered the first "near-AGI" level model publicly available, setting a new standard for language model capabilities.
</Callout>
