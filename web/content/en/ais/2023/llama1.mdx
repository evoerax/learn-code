## Origin Story

Meta released Llama 1 in February 2023, sparking the open-source LLM revolution. While not the first open LLM, Llama 1's quality at smaller sizes (7B, 13B) enabled local deployment that was previously impossible.

<Callout type="success" emoji="🔥">
Llama 1 enabled the explosion of open-source LLMs — Alpaca, Vicuna, Falcon, and countless others were fine-tuned from Llama weights.
</Callout>
